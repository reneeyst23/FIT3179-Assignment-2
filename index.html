Death Statistics in Malaysia

Death statistics offers not only a complex story of health, and also the socio-economic disparities. This projects dives into the death statistics from 2015 to 2022, exploring the trends that can help inform public health strategies and drive action toward reducing preventable deaths. We will explore how different populations' categories are affected and how we could allocate better healthcare resources to the public.

Death rates was observed in gradual increase throughout the years. If we observe, the state: Perak stood out with have an averagely higher death rate compared to other states. This could be due factors such as heathcare accessibility, or population's lifestyle.

From the graphs, majority of the gender of death cases are Male, slightly outnumbering Female in nearly all states throughout the years. Furthermore, there is a noticeable significant increase from 2020 to 2021 on the total number of deaths, which may be to the COVID-19 outbreak.

Distribution of ethnicity is shown in a ranked bump chart and we could observe Sabah and Selangor are the top 1s overall for all ethnicity. This could mean that the number of populations for all ethnicity are living mostly in these states.

In here we have shown the number of maternal deaths between all states throughout the years. One significant observation is that Selangor has the highest number of maternal deaths compared to other states. This could be due to the population density around that area in which there are many heathcare facilities available for the population to access from. Overall, this visualisation provided insights of death statistics in Malaysia for the recent years, which helped public to understand the trends and patterns of death cases in Malaysia. This could help the government to allocate for better healthcare resources to the public as well as to improve the public health strategies. Morevover, parents could take note on the types of early childhood deaths and take necessary precautions to prevent such cases from happening.
